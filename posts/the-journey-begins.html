The Journey Begins

05/21/2026

The Journey

This is my first official blog post. I got GitHub Pages and Jekyll working well enough to make posts! Now I should probably give some context to this blog. The reason I started this blog, as the Home page says, is to document my journey into learning coding. I started this with basically zero knowledge of programming. I've dabbled in learning Python using Mimo twice. I've cobbled together and edited RGSS2 amd RGSS3 scripts in various RPG Makers with no real idea of what was going on. However, I've never actually had the discipline to sit down and learn coding. Traditional schooling has never been a strong suit of mine, but programming (with a focus on game design) has always been a hobby of mine.

I'll skip the long and boring parts, but recently after moving back to this area, I've realized that a lot of the people I've come to know at my church have backgrounds in computer/IT/programming stuff. After some thoughtful prayer with my wife, I decided to reach out to one of those people who attends my life group (a morning Bible study group before our service). He put together for me a set of modules to get me from zero experience to full-stack development while keeping game development central to the modules.

This blog will track my progress through the modules and hopefully by the end, I'll be ready for a career in programming!

What's Next

At the time of typing this, I've already finished the first two modules, so the next two blog posts will essentially just be retrospective looks at what I've done so far. Module 0 is setting up my dev environment, and module 1 is making a platformer in Godot. Hope to see you in the next post!
